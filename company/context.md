# Company Context

## Company
Oddinks — "We build odd things for the internet."

## Mission
Build privacy-focused tools and indie platforms. Ship functional products, stay bootstrapped, stay sustainable.

## Guiding Principles
- Privacy as a core feature, not an afterthought
- Rapid iteration over perfectionism
- Straightforward technology without hype
- Bootstrap funding — sustainability over growth-at-all-costs
- Privacy-by-default design choices

## Current Strategic Priorities
- Shipping and iterating on existing products
- Maintaining bootstrapped operations
- Building sustainable revenue without venture capital

## Team & Roles
Solo founder/builder based in California. Wears all hats — engineering, product, design, marketing, support.
