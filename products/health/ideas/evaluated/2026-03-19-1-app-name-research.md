## Top 3 Recommendations

### 🥇 1. Grove
**grove.app — AVAILABLE** | grove.com — taken (Grove Collaborative, cleaning products)

Natural, growth-forward, wellness-adjacent. Easy to spell, pronounce in any language. The nature metaphor (growth, nourishment from the ground) fits both nutrition and fitness without needing explanation. Privacy angle: "your grove" = a private place that's yours.

- App Store: No health/fitness app named Grove. "Grove Collaborative" (home cleaning products) exists on the store but is a different category — low conflict risk.
- Domain: grove.app ✅, grove-wellness.com ✅, getgrove.com (unverified)
- Social: @grove handles likely taken but @groveapp / @getgrove probable
- Trademark risk: Grove Collaborative is a real brand (home products). Not direct competition but worth a trademark search before committing.
- **Best for:** Leaning into natural/wellness positioning

---

### 🥈 2. Rova
**rova.app — AVAILABLE** | rova.com — taken

Coined word. Clean, modern, no inherited meaning and no inherited baggage. Four letters, two syllables, easy to spell phonetically in English, Spanish, French, and most European languages. The "r" and "v" give it a strong, slightly technical feel — subtly aligned with the privacy/security angle without being heavy-handed.

- App Store: No "Rova" app found in health/fitness — essentially clear
- Domain: rova.app ✅, rova.fit ✅, rova.care ✅
- Social: @rova and @rovaapp likely available (no prominent social presence found)
- Trademark risk: Low — coined word with no existing major brand
- **Best for:** Clean brand slate, privacy-first positioning, long-term flexibility

---

### 🥉 3. Keel
**keel.app — AVAILABLE** | keel.com — taken

"Even keel" = balance, stability, equilibrium. This maps directly to the health proposition: balanced nutrition, consistent workouts, sustainable habits. Short, clean, one syllable, strong consonants. keel.app is available.

- App Store: "Keel Habits" (habit tracking, different focus), "Even Keel" (spa scheduling), "Keel Mind" (mental health) — some name-space competition but no direct nutrition+workout competitor
- Domain: keel.app ✅, keel.fit ✅, keel.health (unverified)
- Social: @keelmind is taken; @keelapp probably available
- Trademark risk: "Keel Virtual Health" (keelwell.com) and "Keel Mind" exist — worth checking USPTO before committing. The concept is used but not heavily trademarked in consumer health apps.
- **Best for:** Metaphor-driven brand (balance/stability), memorable one-word
