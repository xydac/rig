# Decision: App Name — Keel

**Date:** 2026-03-19
**Product:** Health → Keel
**Status:** Decided

## Decision
The health app is named **Keel**.

## Domain
- **keel.app** — available, needs purchase
- keel.com — taken (unrelated)
- keel.health — taken (B2B mental health training platform, low conflict)
- keelapp.com — taken (home construction, no conflict)

## Social
- @keelapp — likely available on X and Instagram

## Trademark
- No consumer health/fitness app holds "Keel" trademark in US
- Closest: Keel Digital Solutions (Canadian B2B clinical platform) — different market
- Formal USPTO clearance search recommended before filing ($300-500)

## Rationale
- "Even keel" = balance/stability — strong metaphor for health app
- Short, memorable, international
- Clean .app domain available
- Low trademark conflict in consumer health/fitness space
